About ISRO

The Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) is the national space agency of India, operating under the Department of Space. ISRO was formed in 1969 and has since been pioneering space research and satellite deployment for a variety of applications. Its vision is to "harness space technology for national development, while pursuing space science research and planetary exploration." Major achievements include the Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan), Chandrayaan lunar missions, and commercial satellite launches via PSLV.
